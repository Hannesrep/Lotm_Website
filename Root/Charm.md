
**Type:** Active — Social Enchantment / Witch  
**Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot  
**Range:** Presence / Interaction  
**Duration:** 10–30 Minutes (GM discretion)

**Description:**  
The Witch enhances their supernatural allure, presence, and charisma. Their appearance becomes mesmerizing, making others instinctively trust, admire, or lower their guard around them.

**Effects:**

- Advantage on persuasion, deception, seduction, and manipulation checks
    
- Targets become more trusting and receptive
    
- Reduces suspicion during infiltration or social encounters
    
- Weak-willed targets may hesitate to attack initially
    

⚠ Charm influences perception — not full mind control.
