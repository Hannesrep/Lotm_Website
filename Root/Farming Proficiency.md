**Type:** Passive — Agricultural & Tool Mastery  
**Pathway:** Planter  
**Activation:** Permanent

**Description:**  
Planters possess refined agricultural knowledge and natural affinity toward cultivation. Their understanding of soil, seeds, growth cycles, and farming tools far exceeds that of ordinary farmers.  
Years of labor also grant them practical combat capability using farming equipment as improvised weapons.

## **Agricultural Effects**

- Proficiency with all standard farming tools
    
- Can accurately identify seed types, plant species, and growth stages at a glance
    
- Instinctively recognize healthy vs. diseased crops
    
- Reduced planting and harvesting time
    
- Increased crop yield and plant survival rate under normal conditions
    
- Advantage on checks related to agriculture, soil analysis, and plant care
    

## **Improvised Weapon Mastery**

Planters treat farming equipment as martial tools in combat.

- Farming tools (scythes, sickles, hoes, pitchforks, shovels, flails, etc.) count as proficient weapons
    
- +2 bonus to attack rolls when using farming tools as weapons
    
- +2 bonus to damage rolls with farming equipment
    
- Farming tools lose the “improvised” penalty
    
- Critical hits with bladed farming tools deal an additional **+1d6 damage**
    

## **Combat Adaptation**

- Advantage on contested rolls to resist being disarmed while wielding farming equipment
    
- Can switch between tool-use and weapon-use without action cost
    

## **Limitations**

- Bonuses apply only when using farming equipment
    
- Does not grant proficiency with standard military weapons
