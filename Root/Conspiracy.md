**Type:** Active — Manipulation / Mental Influence  
**Pathway:** Conspirer  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 30–50 meters (Line of Sight / Social Interaction)  
**Duration:** Instant / Short (1–2 rounds)

**Description:**  
Conspirers manipulate deception, misdirection, and confusion to control events and influence targets. Through observation, careful speech, and calculated acts, they manipulate enemies’ perceptions, choices, and actions.

**Effect:**

- Causes targets to misjudge allies, actions, or intentions
    
- Induces **confusion or hesitation**, potentially making enemies vulnerable
    
- Can manipulate social interactions, enabling sneak attacks, ambushes, or political advantage
    

**Mechanics:**

- Target makes a Wisdom save (Seq 8: DC 14, Seq 7: DC 15)
    
- On failure, target suffers **disadvantage on attacks and skill checks** for 1 round
    
- May allow allies to gain advantage on attacks or skill rolls against affected targets
    

**Limitations:**

- Only works on sentient beings aware of the Conspirer
    
- Effects scale with Sequence and proximity of allies
