**Type:** Active — Ritual Curse Magic / Witch  
**Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** Connection-based

**Description:**  
Through a personal medium (blood, hair, flesh), the Witch establishes a mystical link to a target and inflicts internal spiritual damage or catastrophic misfortune.

**Effects:**

- Burn medium with Black Flames → internal damage to target
    
- Persistent weakening effects
    
- Long-range curse application
    
- Can escalate into catastrophic fate curses via rituals
    

⚠ Targets can sever the connection through purification or substitution abilities.
