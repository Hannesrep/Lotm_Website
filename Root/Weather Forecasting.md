**Type:** Active — Environmental Perception  
**Pathway:** Weather-aligned / Observation-based Pathways  
**Range:** Regional (up to several kilometers depending on visibility)  
**Duration:** Instant analysis / Predictive window up to 24–72 hours

**Description:**  
Through observation of clouds, wind currents, air pressure shifts, humidity patterns, and subtle natural fluctuations, the user can predict upcoming weather developments with supernatural accuracy.

## **Effect**

- Predict rain, storms, temperature shifts, fog, or clear skies
    
- Estimate arrival time and duration of major weather changes
    
- Detect unnatural or magically influenced weather disturbances
    

## **Mechanics**

- Grants advantage on Survival, Navigation, or environmental analysis checks
    
- Allows preparation against incoming storms or hazardous climate shifts
    
- Can identify inconsistencies in natural patterns that suggest supernatural interference
