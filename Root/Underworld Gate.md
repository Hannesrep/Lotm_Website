**Type:** Active — Spirit/Undead Manipulation / Gatekeeper  
**Pathway:** Gatekeeper  
**Availability:** Seq 6–5  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 10–20 meters (targeted)  
**Duration:** Concentration / Scene-based

**Description:**  
The user can **sense, summon, and manipulate gates to the Underworld**, controlling the flow of spirits and undead. The gate acts as a terrifying portal capable of pulling targets in and disrupting the battlefield.

⚠ _“Step too close, and the dead will claim you.”_

### **Mechanics**

- main target pulled in with full suction; nearby targets slowed or hindered
    
- Gate generates suction capable of clearing airborne hazards (poisons, fogs)
    
- Can absorb certain magical “fog” effects, 
- if target is pulled into the gate, target will disappear for one round and take 4d10 dmg (vllt anders)
    

**Limitations:**

- Targets can attempt a Strength or Dexterity save to resist being pulled but looses initiative
    
- Requires line of sight or awareness of target’s location
