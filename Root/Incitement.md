**Type:** Active — Mental / Social Manipulation  
**Pathway:** Conspirer  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 10–30 meters (Verbal or subtle cues)  
**Duration:** Instant / 1 round

**Description:**  
An advanced form of Provocation. The Conspirer actively incites desires, rage, or violent thoughts within targets’ minds, causing temporary loss of control or irrational actions.

**Effect:**

- Increases effectiveness of social or combat manipulations
    
- Induces **anger, desire, or compulsive behavior**
    
- Targets may attack others, flee, or act rashly
    

**Mechanics:**

- Target makes a Wisdom or Charisma save (Seq 8: DC 15, Seq 7: DC 16)
    
- On failure, target attacks nearest hostile or performs irrational action for 1 round
    
- Can be combined with Conspiracy for stronger effect
    

**Limitations:**

- Only works on sentient creatures capable of complex thought
    
- Duration and potency increase with Sequence
