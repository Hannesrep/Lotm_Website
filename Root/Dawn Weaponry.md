**Type:** Active — Offensive Manifestation / Dawn Paladin  
**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot

**Description:**  
The Paladin condenses sacred dawn light into powerful weapons — most notably the **Sword of Dawn**, a purification-imbued weapon capable of devastating corrupted enemies.

### **Effects**

- Create greatswords, axes, spears, or daggers made of dawn light
    
- Attacks inflict purification damage to evil, undead, and corrupted beings (3d6)
    
- Generates powerful gusts capable of destabilizing enemies
    

### **Hurricane of Light (Special Attack)**

- Activated by shattering the Sword of Dawn
    
- Creates a violent, cutting storm of sacred fragments (spherical, 10 feet, doesnt dmg allies) (4d12)
    


**Cost (Special Rule):**

- **No spell slot required**
    
- Consumes significant HP instead (half)
    
- Cant use dawn weapons again (long rest)
    

### **Scaling**

- **Seq 6:** 3d6
    
- **Seq 5:** 3d8
