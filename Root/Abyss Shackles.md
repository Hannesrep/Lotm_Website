**Type:** Active — Control / Darkness Binding  
**Pathway:** Vampire  
**Sequence:** 7–5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 25 meters  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 5 minutes

**Description:**  
The Vampire condenses nearby darkness into **illusory shackles** that bind and restrict enemies.

---

### **Scaling**

**Seq 7**

- Restrains one target if they fail a Will/Strength check.
    
- Movement reduced to zero for 1 round.
    

**Seq 6**

- Can bind up to 3 targets.
    
- Shackles deal 2d8 shadow damage per round while restraining.
    
- Targets suffer disadvantage on physical actions.
    

**Seq 5**

- Up to 5 targets bound simultaneously.
    
- Shackles deal 3d10 shadow damage per round.
    
- Can partially control restrained enemies’ movement (drag or force kneel).
    
- Darkness-heavy environments increase binding strength.
