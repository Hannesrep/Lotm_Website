**Type:** Passive — Mental Fortitude

**Description:**  
The user’s mind is hardened against supernatural fear, eldritch horror, and overwhelming psychological pressure. Exposure to terrifying entities, disturbing scenes, or spiritual intimidation has significantly reduced impact. The user maintains clarity of thought and emotional stability even in situations that would normally cause panic or mental collapse.

**Effects:**

- Advantage on saving throws against fear, terror, madness, and intimidation effects
    
- Immune to minor supernatural fear auras
    
- Cannot be forced into panic or paralysis from horror alone
    
- Maintains decision-making under extreme psychological stress
