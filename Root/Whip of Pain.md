**Type:** Active — Soul Torture / Control  
**Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 15 meters  
**Action:** Action

**Description:**  
A whip of psychic lightning lashes the target’s Soul, inducing intense neurological and spiritual agony. Primarily used to suppress resistance during interrogation.

---

### **Base Mechanics**

- Target makes a WIS/Will save
    
- On failure → full effect
    
- On success → half damage, no control effect
    
- Damage is non-lethal but suppressive
    

---

### **Damage Scaling**

**Seq 7 — Agonizing Strike**

- **3d8** psychic damage
    
- Target trembles (−2 to next action)
    

**Seq 6 — Crushing Torment**

- **4d10** psychic damage
    
- Target forced to kneel or lose action on failed save
    

**Seq 5 — Absolute Submission**

- **5d10** psychic damage
    
- Target cannot lie or resist questioning for 1 round (failed save)
    
- Severe trembling / prone check
    

---

### **Limitations**

- Not lethal
    
- Strong-willed targets can resist
    
- Ineffective against mindless undead constructs
