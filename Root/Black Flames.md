**Type:** Active — Chaos Flame Sorcery / Witch  
**Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 30–60 m

**Description:**  
The Witch conjures chaotic Black Flames that burn Spirituality rather than physical matter. These flames can harm spirits, bypass conventional defenses, and detonate within targets after contact.

**Effects:**

- Spirit-damage fire (affects Wraiths, Souls, Spiritual Constructs)
    
- Remote detonation after successful hit
    
- Cannonball-like compressed flame projectile
    
- Functions underwater
    

⚠ Strong vs spiritual entities — weaker against purely physical constructs.
