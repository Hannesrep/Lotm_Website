**Type:** Passive — Perception / Corpse Collector

**Description:**  
By partially closing or pressing on their eyes, the user can activate a unique perception mode where their eyes turn gray and then colorless. This grants the ability to analyze and discern the weaknesses of **undead and spirit creatures**, even those previously unknown. It functions as a supernatural analytical sense.

**Effects:**

- Automatically identifies vulnerabilities of undead and spirits within line of sight
    
- Advantage on perception, insight, or investigation checks related to creatures’ weaknesses
    
- Provides tactical information for combat, restraint, or neutralization
    
- Always available; no spell slot or activation cost
    

**Limitations:**

- Only works on undead or spiritual entities; cannot detect mundane weaknesses
    
- Requires visual line to target; cannot perceive through walls or obstacles
    
- Continuous use may cause eye strain or fatigue (GM discretion)
