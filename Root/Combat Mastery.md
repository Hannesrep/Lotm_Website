**Type:** Passive — Martial Skill 

**Description:**  
Warriors have mastered the fundamentals and advanced forms of martial combat. They can learn and adapt to any fighting style, stance, or martial discipline with ease, seamlessly integrating techniques into their personal combat flow.

**Effects:**

- No disadvantage when switching between different fighting styles or combat stances
    
- Advantage on grappling, disarming, parrying, or close-combat maneuver checks
    
- Terrain instability, unconventional combat positions, or unusual fighting environments do not impose stance penalties
    

**Combat Impact:**

- Improved efficiency in unarmed combat
    
- Greater adaptability in unpredictable battles
    

⚠ **Warning:** Overconfidence in physical mastery can make warriors vulnerable to purely spiritual, psychological, or conceptual attacks.
