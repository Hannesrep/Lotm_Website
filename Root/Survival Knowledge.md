**Type:** Passive — Tactical Survival Expertise / Hunter Pathway

**Description:**  
Upon consuming the Hunter potion, the individual gains instinctive knowledge of wilderness survival, medicinal plant use, animal anatomy, and practical toxin application. This knowledge is precise, actionable, and combat-relevant.

## Core Effects

### 1️⃣ **Field Medicine**

- Can identify natural haemostatic plants and emergency medicinal resources
    
- Can stabilise bleeding wounds without tools
    
- Can create improvised bandages, poultices, or coagulation mixtures
    
- Gain advantage on survival-based healing checks
    

**Combat Use:**

- Stop bleeding effects
    
- Reduce ongoing damage
    
- Stabilise dying allies
    

### 2️⃣ **Toxin Knowledge**

- Identify naturally occurring poisons
    
- Extract toxins from plants or animal organs
    
- Safely apply poison to weapons
    

**Effects:**

- Weapon Poison (Duration: 3 Hits or 1 Scene)
    
- Minor Constitution check for target
    
- On fail: Damage over Time or debuff (GM defined)
    

### 3️⃣ **Anatomical Precision**

- Deep knowledge of animal and humanoid weak points
    
- Efficient harvesting of useful organs
    
- Improved ability to field-dress creatures
    

**Combat Impact:**

- Minor bonus to tracking injured targets
    
- Bonus to finishing blows against beasts
    
- Better resource harvesting post-fight
