**Type:** Passive (Active Activation) — Defensive Enhancement  
**Pathway:** Spectator  
**Activation:** Action — No Spell Slot Required  
**Duration:** 1 hour per activation then after long rest

**Description:**  
Dragonscales grants the user the ability to activate a protective spiritual layer resembling the resilience of dragon scales. Once activated, the effect lasts for one hour, improving physical defense, resilience, and stability. While the ability is passive in nature, it requires conscious activation to trigger its protective properties.

### **Effects**

- **Physical Defense:** +2 to AC or physical damage resistance (GM discretion)
    
- **Endurance:** +2 to Constitution-based checks and saving throws
    
- **Strength & Dexterity:** Minor enhancement to Strength and Dexterity-based rolls (+1 bonus)
    
- **Stability:** Cannot be knocked prone by environmental factors or minor physical attacks
    
- **Durability:** Reduces impact of falls or blunt attacks (GM may assign fixed damage reduction, e.g., 1d4 per hit)
    

### **Limitations**

- Can only be active for **1 hour per activation**
    
- Requires conscious activation (cannot trigger automatically)
    
- Does not protect against magical or conceptual damage unless GM rules otherwise
