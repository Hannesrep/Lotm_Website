**Type:** Active — Spiritual Weapon Manifestation  
**Cost:** 1 Level 1 Spell Slot (per conjuration or per encounter)  
**Activation:** Instant

**Description:**  
Paper Daggers allow the user to transform ordinary paper or symbolic paper constructs into spiritually reinforced throwing weapons. Once infused with spirituality, the daggers become sharp, fast, and capable of harming both physical targets and low-level spiritual entities. Their effectiveness depends on the user’s focus, precision, and spiritual control rather than physical strength.

### **Damage by Sequence**

| Sequence | Damage         |
| -------- | -------------- |
| Seq 9    | 1d4 + Modifier |
| Seq 8    | 1d6 + Modifier |
| Seq 7    | 1d8 + Modifier |
| Seq 6    | 2d6 + Modifier |
| Seq 5    | 3d6 + Modifier |

- **Modifier:** User’s relevant mental/spiritual attribute (Spirituality)
    
- **Attack Roll:** Use standard D20 + Bonus - Sanity
    
- **Range:** Shortrange and medium range (0–30 ft)
    

### **Core Uses**

- Quick ranged attacks
    
- Strike low- to mid-level spiritual entities
    

### **Drawbacks & Risks**

- **Spiritual Drain:** Repeated use causes mental fatigue and reduced precision
