**Type:** Active — Toxic / Ranged Manipulation  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 7–5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 15–30 m  
**Duration:** Instant

**Description:**  
The Druid can fling strands of their own hair, which combusts spontaneously into a **highly toxic black substance**, inflicting poison damage and debuffing targets.

**Mechanics:**

- **Damage:** Poison (Seq 5: 2d8).
    
- **Effect:** Target must make Constitution save or be poisoned for 1 round (disadvantage on attacks, checks, and movement slowed by half).
    
- Can target multiple enemies if hair strands are split (GM discretion).
    
- Toxic substance lingers briefly on the environment, creating hazardous zones for enemies.
