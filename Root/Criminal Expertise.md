**Pathway:** Prisoner

**Description:**  
Upon advancement, a Prisoner gains an ingrained body of knowledge that elevates them beyond a mere convict into a master of confinement survival and criminal methodology.

This knowledge is instinctive and experience-like, allowing them to understand structural weaknesses, improvised tools, and covert lethality without formal instruction.

---

### **Core Competencies**

**1. Escape Mastery**

- Identify structural weaknesses in cells, restraints, and bindings.
    
- Improvise lockpicking tools from mundane materials (e.g., wire, scrap metal).
    
- Conceal tools and contraband effectively.
    
- Devise tunnel systems or structural bypasses using minimal equipment.
    

**2. Improvised Lethality**

- Convert harmless objects into lethal weapons.
    
- Exploit anatomical weak points efficiently.
    
- Execute silent takedowns with minimal resources.
    

**3. Criminal Technique Proficiency**

- Skilled in sabotage, infiltration, and evasion.
    
- Knowledge of concealment methods and evidence removal.
    
- Capable of manipulating confined environments to their advantage.
    

**4. Explosives Handling**

- Understand bomb construction principles and detonation mechanisms.
    
- Assess structural blast impact zones.
    
- Safely handle and deploy improvised or standard explosives.
    

---

### **Limitations**

- Knowledge does not automatically grant physical strength or supernatural enhancement.
    
- Requires access to materials to fully exploit expertise.
    
- Highly secure, mystical, or spiritually reinforced prisons may still require additional abilities to escape.
