**Type:** Passive — Perfect Recall & Target Lock  
**Pathway:** Arbiter  
**Sequence:** 8

---

### **Description**

If a Sheriff has seen a person directly, in a photograph, or even in a sketch, they permanently memorize the target’s appearance with extraordinary precision.

This is not simple memory, but a spiritually reinforced imprint.

---

### **Effects**

- Perfect recall of facial features and physical traits.
    
- Ability to identify disguises with minor alterations.
    
- Gains a subtle extraordinary sense pointing toward the memorized individual when nearby.
    
- Detects abnormalities and faint traces connected to the recognized target.
    

This allows efficient investigative sweeps and suspect filtering.
