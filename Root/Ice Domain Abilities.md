### **Contact-Freeze**

Physical contact instantly chills target tissue.

- Freezes blood flow locally.
    
- Causes stiffness and slowed movement.
    
- Stronger with prolonged contact.

### **Ice Stun**

Encases a target in solid ice.

- Immobilizes target.
    
- Preserves bodily functions while sealed.
    
- Requires short channeling window.

### **Ice Wall Formation**

Freezes nearby water into defensive structures.

- Instant barricade creation.
    
- Can reshape terrain defensively.
    

---

### **Ice Slipping Area**

Creates thin frozen layers on surfaces.

- Reduces mobility of enemies.
    
- Effective for battlefield control.

### **Frozen Atmosphere**

Lowers ambient temperature.

- Generates chilling winds and snowfall.
    
- Interferes with movement and visibility.
