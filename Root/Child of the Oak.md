**Type:** Active — Self-Buff / Protection  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** Self  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

**Description:**  
The Druid can create a **layer of stone or wood-like skin** on their body, greatly enhancing defense while maintaining full mobility.

**Mechanics:**

- **Damage Reduction:** Reduces incoming physical damage by 5–10 (GM discretion).
    
- **Protection:** Provides resistance against bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing attacks from non-magical sources.
    
- **Mobility:** Movement is unaffected.
    

**Limitations:**

- Concentration required; cannot cast other concentration spells simultaneously.
    
- Does not protect against magical attacks or environmental hazards unless enhanced.
