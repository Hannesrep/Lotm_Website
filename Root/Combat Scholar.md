**Type:** Passive — Martial Enhancement  
**Pathway:** (Custom / Compatible with tactical pathways)  
**Activation:** Permanent

**Description:**  
Through analytical combat study and supernatural battle awareness, the Combat Scholar optimizes close-quarters engagements. Their strikes are calculated, efficient, and aimed at maximizing tactical advantage.

## **Core Effects**

- +2 bonus to melee attack rolls
    
- +2 bonus to melee damage rolls
    
- Gain proficiency (or expertise, if already proficient) in one melee weapon type
    
- Advantage on checks to analyze opponent fighting style after 1 round of observation
    

## **Adaptive Combat Analysis**

After engaging the same target for 1 full round:

- Gain +1 cumulative bonus to melee attack rolls against that target (max +3)
    

## **Limitations**

- Bonuses apply only in melee range (5–10 ft depending on weapon)
    
- Adaptive bonus resets if target changes
    
- Does not apply to ranged attacks or spell damage
