**Type:** Active — Soul Surgery  
**Pathway:** Doctor  
**Range:** Touch  
**Duration:** 1–3 rounds

**Description:**  
The Doctor performs surgery directly on the Soul. This can mend spiritual wounds, remove corruption, or be used offensively to sever the Soul.

## **Healing Mode**

### Soul Restoration:

- **Seq 8:** Restore minor soul damage
    
- **Seq 7:** Restore moderate spiritual fracture
    
- **Seq 6:** Restore severe soul damage
    
- **Seq 5:** Fully mend fragmented astral structures
    

Healing potency increases significantly beyond Evil Ailment Treatment.


## **Offensive Mode**

Requires direct touch and partial surrender of mental defense.

### Damage Scaling:

- **Seq 8:** 4d10 Psychic / Soul Damage
    
- **Seq 7:** 6d10 Psychic
    
- **Seq 6:** 8d10 Psychic
    
- **Seq 5:** 12d10 Psychic
    

If target’s mental defenses are lowered, damage ignores resistances.

At Seq 5, Doctor may:

- Temporarily sever emotional constructs
    
- Suppress specific desires for 1 minute
    
- Permanently remove minor emotional traits (GM discretion)
