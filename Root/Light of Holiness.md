## **Light of Holiness**

_(Upgrade of Holy Light)_  
**Type:** Active — Divine Radiance / Sacred Manifestation  
**Pathway:** Sun / Bard
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 60 feet summon, One Zone can be 10 feet large, at once only 3 Zones 
**Duration:** 5 combat rounds

**Description:**  
Light of Holiness summons concentrated divine radiance embodying sacred authority. The light strengthens allies’ spiritual power and weakens unholy or corrupted entities. Unlike normal Holy Light, this manifestation carries conceptual sanctity — it reinforces faith, oaths, and divine-aligned actions.

### **Core Mechanics**

- Creates a radiant zone or descending pillar of holiness
    
- Allies within light:
    
    - Bonus to attacks
        
    - Advantage on mental resistance and morale checks
        
    - Increased effectiveness of holy or oath-based abilities
        
- Enemies within light:
    
    - Radiant damage each round  (After first hit dont need to roll again for these zones: 3d6 and always crit undead)
        
    - Disadvantage on corruption or shadow abilities
