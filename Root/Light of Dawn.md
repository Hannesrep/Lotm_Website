**Type:** Active — Sacred Field / Dawn Paladin  
**Availability:** Seq 6–5  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Radius:** ~40 feet 
**Duration:** Instant / Scene-based

**Description:**  
The Dawn Paladin releases a sacred sunrise radiance that fills the battlefield with purifying light. It disrupts shadows, dispels concealment, weakens evil entities, and stabilizes corrupted environments.

### **Effects**

- Dispels illusions, invisibility, concealment, and shadow-based effects
    
- Weakens undead, wraiths, and corrupted entities
    
- Purifies mystical pathogens and reduces environmental corruption
    
- Interferes with disturbances and supernatural fog
    

**Limitations:**

- Strong terrain users may overpower the effect
