**Pathway:** Prisoner → Lunatic → Werewolf → Zombie  
**Sequence:** 7  
**Activation:** Action  
**Duration:** Sustained (high Spiritual cost)

---

## **Core Undead Physiology**

Upon advancement, the Beyonder becomes a special, high-grade Zombie — an undead entity with extreme defense, strength, and spell-like capabilities.

### **Defensive Overhaul**

- Body hardness comparable to steel.
    
- Standard revolver shots to the head cause dizziness at most.
    
- Requires repeated concentrated damage to breach skull-level defenses.
    
- Physical damage is non-lethal unless the head is destroyed.
    
- Immunity to fire, drowning, and many conventional magical effects.
    

**Weaknesses:**

- Purification and Sun-aligned abilities deal amplified damage.
    
- Direct attacks targeting the Spirit Body bypass physical durability.
    

---

### **Undead Regeneration**

- Deep lacerations and bone-exposing wounds heal rapidly.
    
- Limb damage recovers unless completely severed and destroyed.
    
- Regeneration weaker under purification influence.
    

---

### **Zombie Strength**

- Strength surpasses prior Werewolf state.
    
- Sprint speed comparable to or exceeding a peak steam locomotive.
    
- Can unleash charged critical blows significantly stronger than normal strikes.
