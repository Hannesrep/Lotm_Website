**Type:** Active — Stellar Manifestation  
**Pathway:** Constellations Master  
**Sequence:** 5  
**Range:** Varies  
**Duration:** Varies

**Description:**  
At Seq 5, the Constellations Master can manifest condensed starlight as structured phenomena. These manifestations possess destructive, restraining, defensive, and mobility properties depending on their configuration.

---

# **Star Pillar**

**Range:** 30 ft radius area  
**Duration:** Instant

**Effect:**  
Multiple manifested stars converge into a descending pillar of concentrated starlight, covering a defined area.

### **Damage**

- **10d10 Radiant / Stellar Damage**
    

### **Additional Effect**

- Structures and non-magical constructs inside the area take double damage
    
- Targets reduced to 0 HP within the area begin structural disintegration (visual collapse effect)
    

---

# **Starry Amber (Starlight Cage)**

**Range:** 60 ft  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

**Effect:**  
Descending streams of starlight form a transparent amber prism around a target, fixing them in place.

### **Damage (On Formation)**

- **6d8 Radiant**
    

### **Restraint Effect**

- Target is fully restrained while contained
    
- The amber has HP equivalent to a high-tier construct (GM discretion)
    
- If destroyed, target is freed immediately
    

---

# **Star Transformation (Stellar Self)**

**Range:** Self  
**Duration:** 1 round

**Effect:**  
The caster’s body transforms into a semi-transparent starlight construct.

### **Effects**

- Gain resistance to physical damage types
    
- Immunity to grapples and restraints
    
- Can pass through narrow openings (no full phasing through solid walls)
    

### **Reformation Burst**

When reforming, starlight surges outward:

- **5d8 Radiant Damage** in 10 ft radius
    

---

# **Star Concealment**

**Range:** 20 ft radius zone  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 5 minutes

**Effect:**  
A field of refined stars conceals and isolates an area.

### **Effects**

- Outside-targeted abilities entering the zone suffer disadvantage
    
- Divination or remote perception attempts into the zone suffer disadvantage
    
- Sound and light distortion within the field
    

No direct damage.

---

# **Starry Shuttle**

**Range:** Self  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

**Effect:**  
Condensed starlight forms beneath the caster, enabling flight.

- Flight Speed: 40 ft
    
- Hover capability
    
- No damage
    

---

# **Star Bridge**

**Range:** 30 ft creation distance  
**Duration:** Concentration

**Effect:**  
Creates a solidified illusory bridge of starlight.

- Supports multiple creatures
    
- Dissolves if concentration breaks
    
- No damage
    

---

# **Star Illumination**

**Range:** 60 ft light radius  
**Duration:** 10 minutes

**Effect:**  
Manifested stars illuminate darkness.

- Counts as bright magical light
    
- Weakens shadow-based concealment (imposes disadvantage on hiding in darkness)
    
- No damage
