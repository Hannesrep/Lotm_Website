**Type:** Active — Summoning / Construct Control  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 30 meters  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

**Description:**  
The Druid can **create a Golem** from natural materials like stone, wood, or earth. The Golem acts as an extension of the Druid’s will, capable of **attacking, defending, or restraining targets**.

**Mechanics:**

- **Size & Strength:** Medium to Large Golem (approx. 3–4 m height), Strength score suitable for heavy attacks.
    
- **Damage:**
    
    - Melee Slam: 3d10 bludgeoning damage per hit
        
    - Optional: Can grapple or push targets instead of dealing damage
        
- **Health:** Seq 5 Golem: moderate durability (GM discretion, suggested HP 60–80)
    
- **Movement:** 15 m per round
    
- **Special:** Can be used to block terrain, shield allies, or trigger environmental effects (falling rocks, crushing hazards).
    

**Limitations:**

- Only **one Golem** can be active at a time.
    
- Requires **line of sight** and stable terrain for creation.
    
- Golem obeys **Druid’s mental commands** for the duration.
