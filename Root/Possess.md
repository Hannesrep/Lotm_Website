**Type:** Active — Marionette Control / Full Possession  
**Requirement:** Seer Pathway — **Sequence 5**  
**Cost:** After successfull SBT
**Activation:** Action  
**Prerequisite:** Target must be fully controlled by **Spirit Body Threads**

**Description:**  
Once a target is completely overtaken by Spirit Body Threads, the user may possess them and convert the body into a controlled marionette. The possessed target acts entirely under the user’s command, allowing the user to utilize the target’s physical abilities and supernatural powers through the puppet body.

While controlling the marionette, the user’s original body remains conscious but limited in capability.

### **Effects**

- Full control over the target’s body and abilities
    
- User may use the target’s skills, combat abilities, and supernatural powers
    
- Target becomes a spiritual puppet under direct command
    
- Control persists till puppet is destroyed even after fight
    

**User Limitations During Possession:**

- Original body may only **walk, move, and perform simple physical actions**
    
- May use mundane tools or weapons (e.g., revolver)
    

### **Limitations**

- Requires fully established Spirit Body Thread control
    
- Only one marionette may be actively possessed at a time
    
- Severe damage to puppet body may break control
    

### **Drawbacks & Risks**

- Heavy mental strain while controlling another body
    
- If the puppet is destroyed, backlash may affect the user
