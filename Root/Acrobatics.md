**Type:** Passive — Physical Trait

**Description:**  
The user possesses exceptional agility, balance, and body control. They cannot be easily thrown off balance by uneven terrain, obstacles, or sudden movements. Their stance and coordination remain steady in nearly any environment, granting a reliable foundation for both combat and exploration.

**Bonuses:**

- No disadvantage on checks due to terrain, balance, or movement challenges
    
- +2 to Dexterity in situations that are influenced by acrobatics (GM decides)
    

⚠ **Warning:** Even the most agile body cannot defy gravity forever — risky leaps or extreme maneuvers may still have consequences.
