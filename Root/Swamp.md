**Type:** Active — Area Control / Terrain Manipulation  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 30 meters radius  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 5 minutes

**Description:**  
The Druid can **turn the ground into swampy terrain**, slowing or restraining enemies within the area.

**Mechanics:**

- **Effect:** All enemies in area must succeed on a Strength check to move normally.
    
- **Movement Penalty:** Failure → Movement halved; difficult terrain; can be restrained if large or heavy targets.
    
- **Terrain Interaction:** Can partially bury enemies if terrain allows (GM discretion).
    

**Limitations:**

- Terrain must be natural or earth-based; cannot create swamp on solid stone/floor.
    
- Concentration required; affected by heavy wind or environmental interference.
