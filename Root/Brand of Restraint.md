**Type:** Active — Suppression Seal / Binding  
**Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 15 meters  
**Action:** Action

**Description:**  
The Interrogator manifests a glowing judicial brand that binds and suppresses the target’s abilities.

---

### **Base Mechanics**

- Target makes STR or Spirit save
    
- On failure → restrained
    
- On success → slowed
    

---

### **Scaling**

**Seq 7 — Binding Mark**

- Restrained for 1 round
    
- −2 to ability activation
    

**Seq 6 — Suppression Seal**

- Restrained up to 2 rounds (concentration)
    
- Supernatural abilities weakened (−2 potency)
    

**Seq 5 — Absolute Edict**

- Full immobilization (1–3 rounds)
    
- Major suppression of Beyonder abilities
    
- Spirit-form entities partially sealed
    

---

### **Limitations**

- Concentration required
    
- High-tier targets may break free
    
- Purification or Law-domain counters can dispel
