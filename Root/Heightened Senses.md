**Type:** Passive — Perception Enhancement / Assassin

**Description:**  
The Assassin possesses razor-sharp hearing and eagle-like visual acuity, allowing them to detect subtle movement, hidden threats, and environmental anomalies.

**Effects:**

- Advantage on perception checks involving sound or visual detection
    
- Increased reaction speed against ambushes (+2 initative on ambush)
    

⚠ _Extremely loud sounds or overwhelming sensory input may temporarily overload perception (GM discretion)._
