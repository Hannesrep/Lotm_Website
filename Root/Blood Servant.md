**Type:** Active — Conversion / Dominance  
**Pathway:** Vampire  
**Sequence:** 6  
**Activation:** Ritual (1–5 minutes)  
**Range:** Touch  
**Duration:** Permanent

**Description:**  
**Blood Servant is the evolved form of Taming.**  
Where a Beast Tamer dominates animals through instinct and fear, a Vampire at this stage extends that dominance to humans through blood and shadow.

The Vampire can convert a weakened or willing human into a **Blood Servant**, binding them through corrupted vitality and shadow essence.

Unlike other conversion methods, this ability **does not require excess Beyonder Characteristics**.

---

### **Effects**

- The Blood Servant becomes absolutely loyal.
    
- Gains enhanced strength and vitality.
    
- Retains personality but cannot betray their master.
    
- Can receive mental commands within moderate range.
    

---

### **Limitations**

- Only a limited number of Blood Servants can be maintained.
    
- Strong-willed individuals may resist if not weakened.
    
- Destruction of the Vampire may destabilize the Servants.
