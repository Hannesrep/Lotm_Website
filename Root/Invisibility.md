**Type:** Active — Concealment Spell / Witch  
**Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Duration:** Minutes (breaks on aggressive actions)

**Description:**  
By scattering enchanted powder and reciting an incantation, the Witch becomes invisible to normal sight.

**Effects:**

- Cannot be seen by naked eye
    
- Advantage on stealth and ambush attacks
    
- Remains partially detectable through strong spiritual perception
    

⚠ Offensive actions may reveal position temporarily.
