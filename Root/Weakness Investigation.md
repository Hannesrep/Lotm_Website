**Type:** Active — Supernatural Perception  
**Pathway:** Reaper  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 60 feet / Target  
**Duration:** Instant / Short (1 round)

**Description:**  
Reapers can discern **vulnerabilities and weak points** in enemies, structures, and supernatural phenomena through supernatural means. This includes physical bodies, non-physical entities, environmental hazards, and mystical barriers.

**Effect:**

- Identifies target’s critical points for **maximizing damage**
    
- Reveals weak spots in structures, barriers, and elemental phenomena
    
- Enables follow-up attacks (like Cull) to exploit identified weaknesses
    

**Mechanics:**

- Requires a successful Perception + Supernatural Knowledge check
    
- Targets must fail a Wisdom save to fully reveal their vulnerabilities
    
- Provides advantage on the next attack targeting weak points
    

**Limitations:**

- Only one target or structure at a time
    
- Effect lasts **until the end of the Reaper’s next turn**
