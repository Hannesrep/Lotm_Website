**Type:** Active — Ranged Spirit Lightning  
**Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 20 meters  
**Action:** Action

**Description:**  
The Interrogator releases bolts of illusory lightning that strike both physical and Spirit Bodies simultaneously.

---

### **Base Mechanics**

- Make ranged spell attack
    
- On hit → hybrid damage
    
- Extra effect vs Spirit-form beings
    

---

### **Damage Scaling**

**Seq 7 — Lightning Lash**

- **3d8** hybrid damage
    
- Wraiths flicker briefly
    

**Seq 6 — Spirit Scorch**

- **4d8** hybrid damage
    
- Spirit entities partially destabilized
    
- −1 to next Spirit action
    

**Seq 5 — Soulbreaker Surge**

- **5d10** hybrid damage
    
- Spirit-form enemies risk forced de-manifestation (CON save)
    
- Brief stun on failed save
    

---

### **Limitations**

- Does not ignore heavy spiritual shields
    
- Less effective against pure physical tanks
