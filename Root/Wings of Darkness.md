**Type:** Active — Mobility / Darkness Manifestation  
**Pathway:** Vampire  
**Sequence:** 7–5  
**Activation:** Bonus Action  
**Range:** Self  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

**Description:**  
The Vampire condenses surrounding Darkness into **illusory bat-like wings**, enhancing mobility and granting limited flight. The darkness can also disperse into bats, black gas, or a spreading black flame.

### **Scaling**

**Seq 7**

- Gain enhanced movement speed (+30%).
    
- Glide and short aerial bursts (up to 10 meters height).
    
- Can briefly disperse into illusory bats to evade (1 round).
    

**Seq 6**

- Controlled flight up to 20 meters height.
    
- Transform into a swarm of shadow-bats (movement + minor harassment damage: 2d6 per round).
    
- Emit spreading black gas that lightly obscures vision.
    

**Seq 5**

- Full maneuverable flight (30m vertical freedom).
    
- Swarm form deals 3d8 necrotic/shadow damage per round.
    
- Can generate a quietly spreading **Black Flame** (3d10 shadow damage, ignores non-magical armor).
    
- Darkness in the area strengthens speed and damage by 20%.
