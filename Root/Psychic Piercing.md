**Type:** Active — Spirit Body Penetration  
**Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 5 meters  
**Action:** Action

**Description:**  
The Interrogator releases concentrated psychic lightning through their eyes, directly piercing the target’s Spirit Body. This attack ignores conventional physical durability and inflicts raw spiritual trauma.

---

### **Base Mechanics**

- Target makes a CON or Spirit save
    
- On failure → full Spirit Damage
    
- On success → half damage
    
- Ignores non-spiritual armor
    
- Leaves target briefly vulnerable (−2 to next defensive roll)
    

---

### **Damage Scaling**

_(Pure Spirit Damage — not physical)_

**Seq 7 — Focused Intrusion**

- **4d8** spirit damage
    
- Minor stagger (0.5 sec disruption)
    

**Seq 6 — Penetrative Shock**

- **5d8** spirit damage
    
- 1 sec stun on failed save
    
- Partial resistance bypass (low-tier Spirit defense ignored)
    

**Seq 5 — Spirit Rupture**

- **6d10** spirit damage
    
- 2–3 sec heavy stun on failed save
    
- Spirit destabilization (−2 to Spirit saves for 1 round)
    

---

### **Limitations**

- 5m maximum range
    
- High-tier Spirit forms may resist stun
    
- Purification shields reduce damage
