**Type:** Active — Support / Buff  
**Pathway:** Bard (Sun Pathway)  
**Spell Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot _(or free if sung as roleplay — GM decision)_  
**Range:** 10 ft 
**Duration:** 5 combat rounds

**Description:**  
Through song, prayer, or rhythmic speech praising the Sun, the Bard empowers allies with courage, strength, and spiritual resilience. The enhancement works by resonating with emotions and faith, strengthening both body and mind while dispelling fear and weakening negative influences. 

### **Core Effects**

- Allies gain bonuses to:
    
    - Strength / physical rolls
        
    - Saving throws vs fear, corruption, darkness, despair
        
    - Mental resistance and morale
        
    
- Removes minor fear or hesitation effects


Bonus: +2
