**Type:** Active — Melee Enhancement / Corrosion  
**Pathway:** Vampire  
**Sequence:** 7–5  
**Activation:** Bonus Action  
**Range:** Self (Melee)  
**Duration:** 5 minutes

**Description:**  
The Vampire’s nails extend and manifest mysterious symbols, allowing them to **cut through steel and corrode defenses**. Particularly devastating against scale, skin, and membrane-based protection.

---

### **Scaling**

**Seq 7**

- Melee attacks ignore non-magical armor.
    
- +2d6 corrosive damage.
    
- Gradual armor degradation.
    

**Seq 6**

- +3d8 corrosive damage.
    
- Reduces target’s physical defense temporarily.
    
- Highly effective against natural armor (double corrosion effect).
    

**Seq 5**

- +4d10 corrosive + necrotic hybrid damage.
    
- Can sever steel or reinforced materials with sustained strikes.
    
- Defensive abilities based on scales, hide, membranes suffer severe weakening.
    
- Targets struck repeatedly risk ongoing corrosion (1d10 per round for 3 rounds).
