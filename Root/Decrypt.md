**Type:** Active — Cognitive / Analytical Ability  
**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  

**Description:**  
Decrypt allows the user to analyze hidden information, decode concealed meanings, identify logical flaws, and dismantle illusions or deceptive constructs. By exploiting inconsistencies the user can uncover truths hidden beneath layers of secrecy, magic, or manipulation.

### **Sequence 7 — Structural Analysis**

**Capabilities:**

- Detect basic illusions and deception attempts
    
- Identify hidden writing, codes, or encrypted messages
    
- Notice inconsistencies in spoken statements or logic
    
- Gain advantage on checks related to lies or disguise detection
    

**Limitations:**

- Cannot fully break advanced illusions
    
- Reveals clues rather than full answers
    

### **Sequence 6 — Magic Recognition**

**Capabilities:**

- Analyze magical effects and find weak points or flaws
    
- Decrypt ritual structures or spell patterns
    
- Reduce illusion effectiveness or partially reveal hidden entities
    
- Gain insight into traps, hidden mechanisms, or supernatural concealment
    
- May grant advantage to allies attacking illusion-based enemies
    

**Additional Effect:**

- On high roll: briefly expose a target’s deception or concealed intent
    

### **Sequence 5 — Absolute Decryption**

**Capabilities:**

- Fully break weaker illusions or false identities
    
- Reveal layered magical concealment and disguised supernatural effects
    
- Identify abilities, hidden skills, or supernatural mechanics affecting a target
    
- Analyze ongoing rituals and predict outcomes
    
- Detect “false reality” effects or manipulated perception
    

**Advanced Effect:**

- May expose logical contradictions or “errors” in abilities, granting temporary vulnerability (GM discretion)
    

## **Tip**
Succesful Decrypt gives advantage on theft, power siphone and steal
