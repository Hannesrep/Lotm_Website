**Type:** Active — Area Hazard / Environmental Manipulation  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 1–50 targets, within 30 meters  
**Duration:** Instantaneous, environmental effect persists up to 1 round (GM discretion)

**Description:**  
The Druid can **designate enemies** and manipulate the surrounding environment to create hazards that target them.

**Mechanics:**

- **Environmental Effects:** Causes rocks, trees, or natural hazards to become unstable and target enemies.
    
- **Target Damage:** 2d10 bludgeoning or piercing damage from falling debris (Seq 5 baseline, adjust per GM).
    
- **Crowd Control:** Can knock prone, restrain, or slow affected targets depending on hazard.
    
- **Scaling:** More natural hazards in the environment → higher damage or additional effects.
    

**Limitations:**

- Requires natural hazards in the area.
    
- Cannot affect structures or enemies outside of natural range.
    
- Environmental hazards are chaotic; GM may adjust effectiveness.
