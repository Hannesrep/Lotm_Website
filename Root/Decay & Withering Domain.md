### **Aura of Decay**

Passive emission during transformation.

- Vegetation withers visibly.
    
- Enemies experience gradual weakening.
    

---

### **Wither Explosion**

Mid-range air detonation into black wisps.

- Inflicts rapid withering effect on impact.
    
- Causes vitality drain over time.
    

---

### **Rotting Body Aura**

Augments all melee attacks.

- Each strike carries Decay property.
    
- Accelerates wound deterioration.
    

---

### **Rotting Sludge Creation**

Creates viscous decaying substance.

- Reduces enemy mobility.
    
- Weakens physical strength over time.
