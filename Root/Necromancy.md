**Type:** Active — Corpse Reanimation / Corpse Collector  
**Pathway:** Spirit Medium  
**Availability:** Seq 6–5  
**Spell Cost:** Level 3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** Self-centered / 10–20 meters  
**Duration:** Scene-based / Controlled by Medium

**Description:**  
Allows the user to reanimate corpses as skeletons or zombies. Reanimated corpses have no independent will or vitality, acting only under the Medium’s commands.

**Mechanics:**

- Seq 6: Animate 1–2 corpses
    
- Seq 5: Animate 3–5 corpses
    
- Corpses follow commands for combat, guarding, or simple tasks
    

**Limitations:**

- Corpses must be recently deceased (GM discretion)
    
- Cannot restore consciousness or original abilities
    
- Large-scale reanimation strains concentration and stamina
    

⚠ **Warning:** Necromancy carries the risk of attracting hostile spirits or spiritual corruption if misused.
