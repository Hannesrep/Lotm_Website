**Type:** Passive — Physical Transformation / Witch  
**Description:**  
By consuming the special potion, a male Witch undergoes a permanent gender transformation into female. This change is biological, physiological, and fully integrated into the body.

**Effects:**

- Complete change of gender to female
    
- Physical characteristics, voice, and appearance adjust accordingly
    
- No inherent impact on abilities, stats, or magical capacity
    

⚠ _This is a permanent transformation!
