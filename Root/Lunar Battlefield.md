**Type:** Active — Domain Expansion / Environmental Control  
**Pathway:** Scarlet Scholar  
**Activation:** Action  
**Range:** 50–100 meter radius  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 15 minutes

**Description:**  
The Scarlet Scholar reshapes the battlefield through manipulation of **Moonlight and Lunar Spirituality**, creating a controlled domain that advantages themselves while suppressing the enemy.

The effectiveness of this ability directly scales with the Scholar’s **understanding of the Moon domain**.

---

## **Modes**

### **1. Full Moon State**

Creates an artificial Full Moon over the battlefield.

**Effects:**

- Spirituality density increases significantly.
    
- Death-aligned and Spirit World effects become stronger.
    
- Spiritual Perception of all entities increases.
    
- Moon-aligned abilities receive amplification.
    

Best used against opponents weak in Spirit World or Spirituality combat.

---

### **2. Moonless Suppression**

Removes or suppresses Moonlight in the area.

**Effects:**

- Reduces lunar amplification effects.
    
- Disrupts Spirit-World resonance advantages.
    
- Denies enemies that rely on Moonlight or Death-based empowerment.
    

Used against spiritually adept enemies.

---

### **Additional Trait**

- The Scarlet Scholar is **immune to the influence of their own Full Moon**.
    
- Lunar Battlefield strengthens all Moon-domain abilities within its area.
