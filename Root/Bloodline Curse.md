**Type:** Active — Ancestral Curse / Witch  
**Cost:** Level 4 Spell Slot  
**Range:** Bloodline Connection

**Description:**  
Using a bloodline-linked medium, the Witch can curse any member of that lineage regardless of distance.

**Effects:**

- Apply curses remotely to all targets connected to the bloodline
    
- Spiritual weakening, misfortune, or progressive decay effects
    
- Can affect multiple family members simultaneously
    
- Extremely difficult to trace back to caster
    

⚠ Requires authentic bloodline medium.
