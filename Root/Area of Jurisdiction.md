### **Description**

A Sheriff maintains a constant metaphysical connection to their officially recognized Area of Jurisdiction.

Within this Territory, their abilities resonate more strongly with order and authority.

---

### **Effects**

- Enhanced effectiveness of all Sheriff abilities within their Territory.
    
- Improved perceptive clarity and environmental awareness.
    
- Faster reaction to disturbances or abnormalities.
    
- Stronger psychological presence when exercising authority.
    

The level of amplification scales with:

- Familiarity with the area
    
- Time spent operating there
    
- Degree of lawful recognition or enforcement influence
    

Outside their Territory, they retain their Beyonder abilities but lose territorial amplification.
