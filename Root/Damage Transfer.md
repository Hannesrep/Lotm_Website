**Type:** Active — Defensive Spiritual Technique  
**Cost:** None  
**Uses:** 1× per Long Rest  
**Activation:** Reaction (player may decide after damage is received)

**Description:**  
Damage Transfer allows the user to spiritually redirect and soften incoming harm that would otherwise incapacitate or kill them. 
**Effect:**

- When receiving damage you may activate Damage Transfer
    
- Reduce the total incoming damage by **50%**
    
- Can be declared **after damage is rolled**
    
- Does not negate status effects unless GM allows
    

**Limitations:**

- Usable only **once per Long Rest**
    
- Cannot be used while unconscious 
    

**Drawbacks & Risks:**

- May leave lingering internal strain or temporary weakness
    
- Repeated exposure to near-fatal damage increases mental instability (GM discretion)
