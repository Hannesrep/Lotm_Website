
**Type:** Active — Structured Magic  
**Pathway:** Warlock  
**Range:** Varies by Spell  
**Duration:** Varies  
**Casting Requirement:** Mental Component + Material Component

**Description:**  
Warlocks construct spells through structured magical execution.  
Each spell requires:

- **Mental Component** — Visualization of the symbolic structure or spoken incantation
    
- **Material Component** — Specific powder, object, or mystical medium
    

Casting requires a full action unless mastery allows partial omission of components.  
Sealed Artifacts may substitute materials if compatible.
