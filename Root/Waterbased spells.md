**Pathway:** Sailor  
**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Range:** 30feet (attack) / Self + shield on skin
**Duration:** Instant for attack / Short for shield = GM Desides :)
**Special:** Can **summon small volume of water** if none present; dissipates after use

## **1. Aqua Jet**

**Description:**  
A high-pressure water strike that launches a concentrated stream at a target. Can be used offensively to strike, push, or destabilize enemies.

**Mechanics:**

- Deals water + physical damage
    
- Can push or knock targets prone depending on seq and save
    
- Small water volume summoned automatically if none present
    

**Scaling by Sequence:**

- **Seq 7:** 1d10 damage, push 5 ft
    
- **Seq 6:** 1d12 damage, push 10 ft, targets make Strength save or fall prone
    
- **Seq 5:** 2d12 damage, push 15 ft, multiple targets in narrow line, prone on failed save
    

**Limitations:**

- Requires line of sight
    
- Only small water volume summoned; massive flood not possible
    
- Targets with resistance to water damage or massive mass may reduce effect


## **2. Water Shield**

**Description:**  
Forms a protective barrier of swirling water around the user, later usable on nearby allies at higher sequences. Reduces incoming damage and can block projectiles or minor attacks.

**Mechanics:**

- Reduces damage from physical and elemental attacks
    
- Blocks small projectiles 
    
- Seq-dependent radius and coverage: Self only → Self + allies
    

**Scaling by Sequence:**

- **Seq 7:** Shield only on self; minor damage reduction (−1d4)
    
- **Seq 6:** Shield on self; moderate reduction (−1d6) 
    
- **Seq 5:** Can extend shield to 1–2 nearby allies; strong reduction (−1d10, blocks minor spells/projectiles


**Limitations:**
- Only small water volume summoned; cannot form massive wall
    
- Shield dissipates after duration / when damage threshold exceeded
