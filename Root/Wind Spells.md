**Type:** Active — Elemental / Wind Manipulation  
**Pathway:** Sailor / Wind‑Blessed  
**Spell Cost:** Level 2–3 Spell Slot  
**Range:** Variable (Self, Melee, Ranged, Area)  
**Duration:** Short duration / Concentration

**Description:**  
Wind‑Blessed Sailors can manipulate air currents to enhance mobility, attack, defense, and perception. The wind is a versatile extension of their body — a force for speed, offense, defense, and observation.

## **1. Wind Surge (Run Speed Enhancement)**

**Effect:**  
Generates wind beneath the feet to boost running speed and agility.

- Doubles base movement (Seq 6)
    
- Triples base movement (Seq 5)
    
- advantage on agility checks
    

## **2. Windblades**

**Effect:**  
Forms invisible, sharp blades of wind to strike at a distance.

- Ranged attack (20–30 meters)
    
- Deals piercing/wind damage (Seq 6: 2d8, Seq 5: 3d8)
    
- Can slice through soft material
    

**Limitations:**

- Requires line of sight
    

## **3. Flight (Short‑Range Flight)**

**Effect:**  
Manipulates wind currents to lift off the ground for short distances.

- Seq 6: Fly up to 10 meters vertically or 20 meters horizontally for one turn
    
- Seq 5: Fly up to 20 meters vertically or 40 meters horizontally for 2–3 turns
    

**Limitations:**

- Cannot sustain long-term flight
    
- wind interference or storms may interrupt
    
## **4. Wind-Infused Strikes** (Bonus Action/ Can be activated with other close combat attacks)

**Effect:**  
Hands and weapons imbued with swirling wind increase penetration and impact.

- Bonus to melee or thrown attacks (Seq 6: +2 to damage, Seq 5: +4 to damage)
    
- Creates gust effect on hit, slightly pushing targets (5 feet)
    

## **5. Eavesdrop via Wind**

**Effect:**  
The user can listen to distant or hidden conversations by directing wind currents to carry sound.

- Seq 6: Hear clearly within 50 meters
    
- Seq 5: Hear clearly within 100 meters; can focus on multiple sources
    
- Can be used for stealth, reconnaissance, or gathering information
    

**Limitations:**

- Strong wind noise or environmental interference reduces clarity
    
- Cannot hear through magical soundproofing
    

## **6. Air Cushion / Soft Landing** (Bonus/Reaction)

**Effect:**  
Generates a cushion of air to reduce impact damage from falls or heavy blows.

- Seq 6: Halves fall or impact damage
    
- Seq 5: Reduces damage by 75%
    

**Limitations:**

- Cannot completely negate damage from extreme heights or massive attacks
