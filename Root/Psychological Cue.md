**Type:** Active — Mental / Combat Manipulation  
**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot  
**Activation:** Action — Target must be observed

**Description:**  
Psychological Cue allows the user to subtly influence a target’s perception or behavior. They may induce hesitation, aggression, or predictability based on body language or emotional state.

**Effect by Sequence:**

In Combat:
- Slight reduction in target’s initiative or reaction
    

Out of Combat:
- If succesfull subtly influence the target

⚠ **Warning:** Overuse may alert the target to manipulation attempts.
