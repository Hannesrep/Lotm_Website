**Type:** Active — Supernatural Perception  
**Pathway:** Mystery Pryer  
**Range:** 60 ft (Seq 9–7) / 90 ft (Seq 6) / 120 ft (Seq 5)  
**Duration:** 10 minutes

**Description:**  
The Mystery Pryer focuses their vision beyond the material layer, perceiving spiritual light, astral distortions, and concealed supernatural traces. This ability enhances analysis and detection but does not grant omniscience.

## **Seq 9**

**Spell Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot

**Effect:**

- See spiritual aura intensity around creatures and objects
    
- Detect presence of illusion or dream distortion in an area
    

**Mechanics:**

- Advantage on Perception or Arcana checks related to illusions or hidden spiritual traces
    
## **Seq 8**

**Spell Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot

**Effect:**

- Perceive outline of Astral Projection
    
- Notice emotional instability through Heart & Mind fluctuations
    

**Mechanics:**

- Advantage on Insight checks
    
- Advantage on rolls to detect concealed entities within line of sight
    
- Can distinguish between mundane concealment and supernatural concealment
    

## **Seq 7**

**Spell Cost:** Level 1 Spell Slot

**Effect:**

- Observe Astral Body structure and Ether Body condition
    
- Identify unstable or weakened spiritual areas
    

**Mechanics:**

- Advantage on checks to analyze supernatural constructs or barriers
    
- Gain +2 bonus on attack rolls targeting spiritually weakened areas (if applicable and previously identified)
    

## **Seq 6**

**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot

**Effect:**

- Perceive layered concealment (illusions + spiritual masking)
    
- Detect mirrored or partially phased entities
    

**Mechanics:**

- Advantage on saving throws against illusion and dream-based effects while active
    
- Can attempt a contested check to reveal concealed beings within range
    

## **Seq 5**

**Spell Cost:** Level 2 Spell Slot

**Effect:**

- Detailed astral structural analysis of one target per turn
    
- Detect corruption or foreign spiritual influence
    

**Mechanics:**

- Advantage on all checks related to analyzing spiritual composition
    
- When targeting an identified weak astral point, gain +2 to attack rolls or spell accuracy
    
- May attempt to expose magically concealed entities through a contested roll
