**Type:** Active — Animal Transformation / Physical Enhancement  
**Pathway:** Druid  
**Sequence:** 7–5  
**Activation:** Action  
**Duration:** Concentration up to 1 hour

**Description:**  
The Druid can transform into a giant bear, roughly **twice the height of a human**, gaining immense **strength, endurance, and natural combat capabilities**. While transformed, their body is adapted for melee combat, enhanced durability, and natural weapon use (claws and teeth).

**Mechanics:**

- **Strength and Endurance:** Doubled relative to human form; allows carrying heavy objects, grappling multiple targets.
    
- **Natural Attacks:** Claws and bite deal significant damage (Seq 5: 3d12).
    
- **Damage Resistance:** Gains +2 to damage reduction against physical attacks.
    
- Cannot use other forms of magical abilities included in the Advancement Ritual while transformed.
