**Cost:** None  
**Activation:** Reaction (player choice)

**Description:**  
Paper Substitute allows the user to replace themselves with a prepared paper figurine at the moment of impact. The incoming attack destroys the substitute while the user instantly reappears a short distance away, avoiding all damage.

**Effect:**

- Negate **all damage** from a single incoming attack or effect
    
- Instantly teleport **10 ft** to a visible nearby location
    
- Consumes **1 Paper Figurine** from inventory
    
- Player decides freely when to activate
    

**Paper Figurine Capacity (by Sequence):**

- **Sequence 7:** 1 Figurine
    
- **Sequence 6:** 2 Figurines
    
- **Sequence 5:** 3 Figurines
    

**Limitations:**

- Requires a prepared Paper Figurine
    
- Must have a valid reappearance space within 10 ft (ex: If aoe effect still usable, but take aoe damage)
    
- Area-wide unavoidable effects may still partially affect the user (GM discretion)
    

**Drawbacks & Risks:**

- Predictable use may allow enemies to anticipate reappearance location
    
- Destroyed figurines must be recreated during rest/preparation
